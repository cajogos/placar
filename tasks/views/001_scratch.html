Introducing Your Team Using Scratch

Now that you have made your team name it is time for you to get to know eachother.
In this task we will be using the skills you have obtained using Scratch at previous CoderDojo sessions or at school.

Coding Task

In this task you will need to use the Scratch Code Editor.

You can also use these Scratch cards to complete this coding task.

Tasks

  • 2 pointsCreate a new Scratch Project
  • 5 pointsAdd a sprite for each member of your team
  • 3 pointsMake each sprite say the name of the member of your team when clicked using your mouse. The "Say Something" card will help you with the say message.
  • 2 pointsBONUS! Make one of your sprites say the name of your Team Leader and make a funny or scary sound!

Physical Task

As a team, perform a 15 second dance that will scare the Witch.
