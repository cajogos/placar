Coding Task

For this task you are encouraged to use any text editor you have on your computer, however for ease of access you can also use the online code editor JSFiddle.

Stuck with your HTML? Check out the awesome tutorials by W3Schools (open it on your research computer).

Stuck with your CSS? Check out the awesome tutorials by W3Schools (open it on your research computer).

Tasks

  • 1 pointCreate your HTML document with the <head> and <body> tags in the correct places.
  • 1 pointCreate a heading with the name of a mythical creature.
  • 2 pointsMake the font of the heading blue.
  • 4 pointsChange the background of your page to be an image using CSS.
  • 2 pointsBONUS! Add a red or green ROUNDED border to the mythical creature name.
  • 2 pointsBONUS! Add a text shadow to the heading.
