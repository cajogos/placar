Make your team's Spooooky Website

Woah! What a scary story that was! In this task we will use our HTML wizardry to create your team's website.

Coding Task

For this task you are encouraged to use any text editor you have on your computer, however for ease of access you can also use the online code editor JSFiddle.

Stuck with your HTML? Check out the awesome tutorials by W3Schools (open it on your research computer).

Tasks

  • 2 pointsCreate your HTML document with the <head> and <body> tags in the correct places.
  • 1 pointCreate a heading with your Team Name.
  • 3 pointsCreate an ordered <ol> or unordered <ul> list with the names of everyone in your team along with their ages.
  • 2 pointsFind an image related to Halloween and add it to your page.
  • 2 pointsBONUS! Add a link to your favourite websites.
  • 2 pointsBONUS! Try to center everything on the page.

Physical Task

Draw, create or even make the scariest creature ever. Give it a name, and make the Witch believe it really does exists so she'll give you presents to go away.
